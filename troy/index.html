Titan Royale (TROY) is a utility-driven digital token built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). It is designed to power a diverse ecosystem that includes Decentralized Finance (DeFi) applications and online gaming. Its core features are a fixed supply to prevent inflation and a focus on real-world utility for its holders.

The maximum and total supply of TROY is fixed at 888,888,888 tokens. No new tokens can ever be created, which makes TROY a deflationary asset by design.

TROY has multiple uses within the ecosystem:

  • Staking & Insurance: You can stake TROY to earn passive income and gain access to a unique "Stake-to-Insure" program offering accidental and living savings coverage.

  • Collateral for Loans: Use other BSC coins and tokens as collateral to borrow TROY, allowing you to access liquidity without selling your other assets.

  • Gaming Currency: TROY will be the primary currency for placing bets, purchasing in-game items, and receiving winnings in partner online gambling and gaming platforms.

  • Governance: Holding TROY gives you the right to vote on important decisions that will shape the future of the project.

The token distribution is transparent and designed for long-term growth:

  • 60% (533,333,333 TROY): Public Circulation for exchange listings, liquidity, staking rewards, and community incentives.

  • 40% (355,555,555 TROY): Owner Reserve for future development, strategic partnerships, and ensuring the project's long-term stability.

Initially, TROY will be listed on PancakeSwap, the leading Decentralized Exchange (DEX) on the Binance Smart Chain. As the project grows, we plan to secure listings on other major centralized exchanges (CEX) like KuCoin and Binance.

Security is our top priority. The TROY smart contract will undergo a comprehensive security audit by a reputable third-party firm. Additionally, a significant portion of our initial liquidity will be locked to protect our investors from volatility.

Our roadmap is divided into key phases, including the launch of our native staking platform, the rollout of the "Stake-to-Insure" and collateralized lending protocols, and the development of our own native Decentralized Exchange (DEX) to serve as the central hub for the TROY ecosystem.
